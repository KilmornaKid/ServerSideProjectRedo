Welcome to Media Madness

Stacks Classic Cinema is here to present you with the blockbusters of the year week to week from Hollywood to Bollywood and what a week to decide to join us on this awesome journey! Firstly, we have the films that will horrify even the most stubborn of you as are Halloween mainstays are still kicking around nearing Christmas though it's like they say it's never too late for a fright night. For those of you unfamiliar with the site we normally run a weekly analysis on our favourite movies showing in our humble establishment, for which links to book tickets are located towards the centre of the pags and of course our current favourite picks are given the honour of domination our best screens with certain movies playing in IMAX or 3D while others have to settle for just the standard screen.

As your likely a new visitor, if you're still reading this that is, let's give you a quick rundown of the site and its various functions and features. The site is primarily focused on the curation of top-quality movies airing in our cinema currently. The home page which you are currently on hosts what we consider the best of the best however if you are hungry for more than that, you can always stop by in person as we tend to host special screenings which cannot be booked through our site.

Although we are a new fledgling cinema we hope to grow quickly and establish a quality cinema experience which casual viewers and movie buffs alike can trust, for which we need your help to establish what our audience would like us to do with the future of the cinema all we need you to do is one simple thing, provide us with as much feedback as possible by stopping by to the cinema in person to tell us about the films new, old, classic all kinds are welcome which you wish to see aired on the big silver screen! Thank you.
