Who are we?

Our new cinema Stack's Classic Cinema is your one stop shop for all the films. Here at Stack's Classic Cinema we pride ourselves on our expertise of all things involving films and we are constantly striving to deliver the best cinema experience possible for you the paying customer. For now, we're a small operation consisting of only several men and women however we're dedicated to showcasing top quality content and our mission statement is to grow the cinema into the number one stop in all of Tralee.

Although we are a new fledgling business operation we hope to grow quickly and establish a quality which viewers can trust, for which we need your help to establish what our audience would like us to do with the future of the cinema all we need you to do is one simple thing, provide us with as much feedback as possible by searching this page you can find our contact details and hopefully you'll tell us what you think and we can improve the experience for everyone! Thank you.
